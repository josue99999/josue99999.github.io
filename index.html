About

Where machines
begin to think.

I work at the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and physical systems — building and training intelligent machines that sense their environment, learn from data, and improve through experience.

My work spans the full stack: from deep learning and computer vision to mechanical design and real-time control. I care about one thing above all — systems that work outside the lab, in the messiness of the real world.

Mechatronics Engineering at UTEC Lima. Researcher at NonHuman. Open to PhD/Master's opportunities in Robotics, Physical AI, and Human-Robot Interaction worldwide.
