About

Robots that learn,
systems that adapt.

I work at the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and physical systems — designing and training intelligent machines that learn from data, interact with their environment, and improve over time.

My work spans the full stack: from reinforcement learning and computer vision to mechanical design and real-time control — always with an eye toward deployment in the real world, not just simulation.

B.Sc. Mechatronics Engineering, UTEC Lima. Currently a researcher and open to PhD/Master's programs in Robotics, Physical AI, and Human-Robot Interaction.
